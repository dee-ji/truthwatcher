# Code of Conduct

Truthwatcher contributors are expected to keep the project welcoming, focused, and respectful.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using inclusive and respectful language.
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
- Keeping technical disagreements focused on evidence and project goals.
- Helping new contributors understand the roadmap and safety model.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or trolling.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly pushing unsafe network automation patterns after maintainers have rejected them.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, discussions, and commits that violate these expectations. Enforcement may include warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository owner profile or other private project contact method. Maintainers should review reports promptly and handle them with appropriate confidentiality.
